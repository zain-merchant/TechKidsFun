Our Mission

O​ur mission is to help kids build STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering and Math) skills while having fun. Tech Kids Fun is all about making kids comfortable with computers, science and technology in a fun, interactive and creative way.​​ We blend the latest in technology with cool hands on science experimentation that create a well-rounded camp experience for kids.


After School Program

These fun classes will help build STEM skills through the medium of logic and programming, as well as through digital experimentation. Children will have a chance to combine their creativity and logic skills to create their animations, projects as well as learn about computer science through fun hands on experiments.
